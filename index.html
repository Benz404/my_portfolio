Assembling a Radio Telescope and Study its Various Implementation and Applications

In this project we aimed to develop a Small-scale amateur Radio Telescope, from an off-set parabolic d2h dish antenna. To proceed with the project, we mount the antenna along with a Low Noise Block (LNB) downconverter device on a German-made equatorial mount. The antenna has the ability to capture the Ku-band (12-18 GHz) of the electromagnetic radiation.

AI-Powered Snake Game: Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Implementation

This project showcases the development of a classic Snake game powered by machine learning and artificial intelligence, supervised by Patrick Loeber. The AI controls the snake's movements, optimizing its performance and decision-making to achieve higher scores and avoid collisions. By employing advanced ML algorithms, the project demonstrates the practical application of AI in game development.

Preliminary Detection of Asteroids in the Indian Sky : in the International Asteroid Search Campaign

In this project we are in the involved in making contributions during the International Asteroid Search Campaign as collaborators in the International Astronomical Search Collaboration (IASC). The focus is on the preliminary detection of multiple asteroids within the Indian sky, highlighting the methodology, results in the broader context of planetary defense and astronomical research.
